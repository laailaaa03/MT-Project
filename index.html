Marrakesh & Ambassadors

Our Project's Story

Dec-12-2023 Head of Ambassadors

Born from Concern, Flourishing with Passion!
In the heart of Marrakech, a group of locals grew worried about tourism's impact on their city's unique charm. They envisioned a different path, one where visitors could truly experience Marrakech's rich heritage while supporting responsible practices.
Thus, the Cultural Tourism Ambassadors of Marrakech were born. With unwavering passion, they set out to create a new model of tourism. They crafted tours that unveiled hidden gems and stories, developed educational materials, partnered with local businesses for authentic experiences, and organized cultural events that brought visitors and locals together.
Their efforts transformed Marrakech's tourism landscape, empowering Marrakchis to shape their city's future. They showed the world that Marrakech was more than just a destination; it was a living, breathing story waiting to be told.
